Zero-cost transformations from sequences of Vn into sequences of arrays.

Safety

The default impls effectively perform transmute, and some of the generic impls assume that it is safe to perform pointer casts between Self and Self::Un. (this may be done even on pointers to pointers, or smart pointers and etc.)
