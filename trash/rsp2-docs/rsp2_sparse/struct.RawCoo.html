Coordinate format.

Elements in arbitrary order. Duplicates are allowed; they are implicitly summed. Great for building a matrix as a sum of terms, but otherwise impossible to work with.

This type is “Raw” because its members are public and its invariants are not protected. It exists to document the “intent” of some fields of a struct, and to provide some simple utility methods.
